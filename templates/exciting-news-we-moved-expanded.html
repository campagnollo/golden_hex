Exciting News! We MOVED & EXPANDED!

From California Sunshine to Triangle Flavors: Golden Hex Expands with European & Mediterranean Delights, becoming the LARGEST European small-business family-owned market in NC!

Calling all foodies and culture enthusiasts in the Triangle! Get ready to embark on a delicious journey across Europe and the Mediterranean, right here in Cary, NC.

The Golden Hex story began with a California family's passion for honey. What started as sharing their love with friends and family soon blossomed into a thriving business, crafting not just the finest raw honey but also pollen, propolis, beeswax candies, natural cosmetics, and soaps.

Upon relocating to North Carolina, we decided to share our love for another kind of sweetness – the vibrant flavors of Europe and the Mediterranean. Our first Cary store, located near Thomas Bond Park, quickly became a haven for those seeking authentic groceries, specialty items, and of course, our beloved North Carolina honey.

Exciting News: Golden Hex Takes Flight to a New Location!

We're thrilled to announce that Golden Hex has expanded its wings and relocated to a new, spacious building at the intersection of 1200 NW Maynard Rd and High House Rd. This move allows us to better serve the Triangle community and offer an even wider selection of European and Mediterranean delights. Our expanded deli section features an impressive selection of cold meats, cheeses, fish, and even red and black caviar. Grab a hot meal or a quick bite from our Hot & Cold Bar. Savor the fresh-baked goodness of our bakery, featuring delectable treats like pierogies, pelmeni, vareniki, cakes, and traditional sweets like halva, marshmallow (zefir), and pastille. Plenty of the choices for vegetarians too.

The Same Dedication to Quality, with Even More to Discover!

At our new location, you'll find everything you loved about Golden Hex from the beginning and added a lot more. Come and explore a vast array of groceries from countries like France, Italy, Greece, Poland, Hungary, Balkan Peninsula, Baltic States and beyond the former Soviet Union, including Armenia, Azerbaijan, Belarus, Estonia, Georgia, Kazakhstan, Kyrgyzstan, Latvia, Lithuania, Moldova, Russia, Ukraine, and Uzbekistan. Find familiar tastes of your childhood from Albania, Bosnia and Herzegovina, Bulgaria, Croatia, Czech Republic, Denmark, Estonia, Finland, France, Germany, Greece, Israel, Kosovo, Liechtenstein, Luxembourg, Portugal, Romania, San Marino, Serbia, Slovakia, Slovenia, Spain, Sweden, Switzerland, Turkey, United Kingdom. Products from over sixty countries are now being represented and we constantly add on more!

More Than Just Food: Home Goods & Gifts

Golden Hex isn't just about food! We also offer a selection of home goods and gifts & souvenirs, so you can add a touch of European or Mediterranean charm to your home or find the perfect present for a loved one.
