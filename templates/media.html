    Specialty Stores: Golden Hex European Groceries & Deli

    Source: Cary Citizen. Feb 8, 2018 by Michael Papich

    Read the full article about Golden Hex store

    "There are a variety of flavors and foods in Eastern Europe and the Mediterranean but they cannot always be found at the grocery store. But a new market opening on Maynard Road will let shoppers try these European foods" (by Michael Papich).

    Food from the Homeland

    Golden Hex European Groceries & Deli , located at 1246 NW Maynard Rd., will feature groceries from Eastern Europe and the Mediterranean. Owner Arthur Schwartz aims to bring the feeling of the homeland with products like sausages, cheeses, fish, and candies from across the continent.

    New Tastes

    Customers can explore shelves filled with Armenian wines, European beers, caviar, Russian dumplings, pierogis, and pickled foods. The deli offers sausages and cheeses made in the Russian and Ukrainian style.
